My Recent Trip to khaler-par

I recently visited Khaler-par, a beautiful place in Bangladesh. It is a small village surrounded by hills and forests. The people there are very friendly and welcoming.
I stayed at a homestay and tried the local food. I also went hiking in the hills and visited a waterfall. It was an amazing experience and I can't wait to go back.

My Favorite Food:
  • Chingri Bhorta

    Chingri Bhorta is a traditional Bangladeshi dish made with shrimp and spices.
    It is a popular dish in Bangladesh and is often served with rice.
    The shrimp is cooked with onions, garlic, ginger, and green chilies, and then mashed into a paste.
    It is a delicious and flavorful dish that is perfect for lunch or dinner.

  • Khichuri

    Khichuri is a traditional Bangladeshi dish made with rice and lentils.
    It is a popular comfort food in Bangladesh and is often served during festivals and special occasions.
    The rice and lentils are cooked together with spices and vegetables to create a flavorful and hearty dish.
    It is usually served with fried eggplant, pickles, and salad.

  • Biriyani

    Biriyani is a popular dish in Bangladesh and is often served at weddings, parties, and other special occasions.
    It is made with basmati rice, meat, and spices, and is cooked in a special way to create a flavorful and aromatic dish.
    The meat is marinated with yogurt and spices, and then cooked with the rice to create a delicious and satisfying meal.
    It is usually served with raita, salad, and pickles.

  • Shorshe Ilish

    Shorshe Ilish is a traditional Bangladeshi dish made with hilsa fish and mustard seeds.
    It is a popular dish in Bangladesh and is often served with rice.
    The hilsa fish is marinated with mustard seeds, turmeric, and green chilies, and then cooked in mustard oil to create a flavorful and aromatic dish.
    It is a delicious and healthy dish that is perfect for lunch or dinner.

  • Chingri Malai Curry

    Chingri Malai Curry is a traditional Bangladeshi dish made with shrimp and coconut milk.
    It is a popular dish in Bangladesh and is often served with rice.
    The shrimp is cooked with onions, garlic, ginger, and green chilies, and then simmered in coconut milk to create a rich and creamy curry.
    It is a delicious and flavorful dish that is perfect for lunch or dinner.
